Yuchen Wu    

I am a postdoctoral researcher in the Department of Statistics and Data Science at the Wharton School, University of Pennsylvania. I received my Ph.D. in 2023 from the Department of Statistics at Stanford University, advised by Professor Andrea Montanari. Prior to Stanford, I received my Bachelor's degree in mathematics from Tsinghua University.

My research lies broadly at the intersection of statistics and machine learning, with a current focus on sampling and diffusion models.
